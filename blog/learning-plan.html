Learning Plan

Here's my plan put in words how I will operate during the Dev Academy programme. The blog is dividedin two sections: Bootcamp learning plan and Foundations learning plan. These reflect my thoughs at different points during the programme. Hopefully they provide insight into my motivations at those points.

Bootcamp Learning Plan


What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I hope the skills I’ve learnt at dev academy would equip me to build personal projects that are relevant and useful in the real world. With these personal projects I’d like to be able to showcase my skills to potential employers. To which I’d like to land my first role in the IT field explore the vast ocean of information and get really technically strong.

I’d like to get to a point where I’d be capable to solving problems through technology, monetize it and influence others to work on that common goal with me.


What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I think my strength to work out solutions to problems independently and critical thinking have been somewhat consistent throughout the foundations. My weakness to raise my hand and ask for help has gotten better since the beginning seeing peers do the same in the discord channels. Making me want to do the same.

I have also realise I would procrastinate on an exercise when I am stuck on an issue and will spend a lot longer on a challenge that I have solved or know I can solve. In saying that I have managed to stay persistent on solving them before moving on by googling to understand the concepts further. I do think I have a long way to go to learn how and how often to ask for help when I am stuck on something.


What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Asking for help would be a challenge for sure specially because during bootcamp I will not have enough time or patience to keep on a problem for longer than expected.

Working in groups has been a challenge in the past and think it could be the same during bootcamp but something I have been constantly and consciously making effort to get better in.


What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

It would be absolutely amazing to grow my skills around teamwork within a group and ultimately my leadership skills. Growing skills around actions that are necessary to motivate myself and others during tough times and even pushing myself and others to excel in day-to-day activities would be great to work on.


What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I would love to have weekly or fortnightly catchups with the bootcamp team to receive feedback and guidance around developing my core and technical skills. These weekly catchups should be 1 on 1 and as a group.

The 1 on 1 catchups would allow deeper and more meaningful conversations around where things stand and how they can be improved even further. The group catchups we currently do within our homegroup have been very good to connect with peers and understand how everyone is feeling and usually knowing that everyone is going through a very similar experience throughout the week.


What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

I’d like to take up the bootcamp team on the technical catchups on a fortnightly or monthly basis to get feedback on how I am going. To come out the bootcamp with skills to figure out and build personal projects and demonstrate my skills to potential employers is my ultimate next goal from the bootcamp. I’ll make sure to be on top of what’s expected and required during each week of the bootcamp. If it meant staying for additional practice or more time to solidify my understanding around concepts I’ll do that.

I’d love to work on feedback that I receive from the bootcamp team as I’d love to be coachable and demonstrate that I am coachable for future employers. I’d like to nail down a system for feedback that really works for me and even be able to structure any kind of feedback and apply that in my day to day.



Foundations Learning Plan


What is your long-term goal or career pathway?

My long-term goal is to own a business that helps solve a problem, at the same time something I am passionate about and am naturally strong in.
To get there I wish to build my fundamental technical knowledge and get an in depth understanding of the industry and all the moving pieces


A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

Something I do very well is being very thorough in my approach, solve problems independently and creatively.
I struggle with are raising my hand to ask for help, being overly critical of my work and needing rational explanations of what I am required to do and why.


What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to be able to work efficiently in a team. As well as build and maintain mutually-beneficial relations with different stakeholders.
