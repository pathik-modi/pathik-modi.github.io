My Learning Plan

Here's my plan put in words how i will operate during the Dev Academy programme.

  1. What is your long-term goal or career pathway?

    My long-term goal is to own a business that helps solve a problem, at the same time something I am passionate about and am naturally strong in.
    To get there I wish to build my fundamental technical knowledge and get an in depth understanding of the industry and all the moving pieces

  2. A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

    Something I do very well is being very thorough in my approach, solve problems independently and creatively.
    I struggle with are raising my hand to ask for help, being overly critical of my work and needing rational explanations of what I am required to do and why.

  3. What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

    I would like to be able to work efficiently in a team. As well as build and maintain mutually-beneficial relations with different stakeholders.

  4. A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include:

    An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives.
    Something I have kept saying to myself in the past is “____ like an athlete”
    It could be study like an athlete or work like an athlete or anything else.
    I plan to do continuous improvements, dedicated and disciplined with my efforts. Improving each day and competing with myself is something that really motivates me.

    What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you
    Raise my hand and ask for help from the facilitator or peers on discord. Putting in the extra hours and not stopping until I catchup

  5. A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

    I commit to seek help from the facilitators on discord channels. I promise to try and find answers to issues myself, search online on google to help me solve a problem. Only after spending 10-15 minutes being stuck will I reach out to the discord channels. I will also provide a good background on what the issues are and what I have done to solve so far.

  6. A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

    I expect the facilitators team to reach out individually or within homegroups to organize regular catchups. Help when someone sends out a problem they are stuck on.
    I also expect them to challenge us to think about solving issues ourselves by providing guidance and advise where needed.

  7. Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things. For example, you might have whānau commitments like school drop-offs. It's important to plan these in.

    Nothing planned on my day-to-day. I am 100% committed in being successful in this new journey and willing to give whatever it takes.
